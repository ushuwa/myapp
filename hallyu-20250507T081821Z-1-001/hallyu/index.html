Discover Us


We're a team of dancers who do covers of various performances, mainly from K-pop and P-pop! While enjoying what we do, we also strive to break barriers within the K-pop/P-pop community. Through our content, we aim to showcase our diversity, talents, and potential, as well as promote a safe space for everyone to express themselves and their love for dance and K-pop/P-pop

Achievements

This category shows the achievements and Hallyu Academy's participations in every events.

ANGAT BUHAY KPOP CONVENTION held at SM City Lucena.

Out of 7 participants on the KPOP Dance Cover Competition, our Stray Kids Dance Cover team perform MANIAC and won 1st place making it our first ever win in a competition.

KPOP STATIC 7: Y.E.S! Year End Special held at Robinsons Galleria.

Out of 17 participants in the KPOP Dance Cover Competition, Hallyu University performed their cover dance of NMIXX and won 3rd place! And out of 45 participants in the Cosplay Category, Xas Xavier Quinn managed to win 3rd place!
